📋 SMS Terms of Service

Program Description

Juvora Life operates an SMS messaging program to notify customers about their orders and provide customer support. By providing your mobile phone number when placing an order, you consent to receive text messages from Juvora Life.

How We Obtain Consent

We only send SMS messages to customers who voluntarily provided their phone number when placing an order through authorized marketplaces including Walmart. Your number is used solely for order-related communications.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your order activity. You may typically receive 1–3 messages per order for status updates or support inquiries.

Charges

Juvora Life does not charge for SMS messages. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

How to Opt-Out

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe immediately. You will receive one final confirmation, and no further messages will be sent. To opt back in, reply START.

Help

Reply HELP to any message, or contact us at support.juvoralife@gmail.com.

Supported Carriers

AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon Wireless, Sprint, Boost Mobile, MetroPCS, U.S. Cellular, and most US carriers. Carrier support is not guaranteed for all carriers.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

  • Your name and shipping address
  • Your mobile phone number (for order SMS only)
  • Order details and purchase history

How We Use Your Phone Number

  • Send order confirmation and status updates
  • Notify you about shipping, delivery, and any order issues
  • Provide direct customer support for your orders

We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party SMS Provider

We use Telnyx (telnyx.com) as our SMS delivery provider. Your phone number is transmitted to Telnyx solely to deliver messages. Telnyx processes this data per their own Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

SMS message logs are retained for up to 12 months for support and compliance. You may request deletion by contacting us.

Your Rights

  • Opt-out of SMS anytime — reply STOP
  • Request what data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
